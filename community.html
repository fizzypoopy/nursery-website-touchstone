Community Involvement

Local Charity

Every season, we contribute our excess plants and produce to the local soup kitchens and homeless shelters.

School Outreach Program

Every month, we schedule a school outreach event with every school in our local district to educate children on the importance of environmental science and conservation efforts that impact our everyday lives and the world around us.

Local Community Cleaning

Every month, we gather together as a community to clean up the trash that may pollute our environment and harm the local wildlife.
